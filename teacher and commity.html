    WHO WE ARE

    We are the members of this awesome academic family. It is a co-educational school with a team of more than 28 dedicated and professional faculties. We ensure that the children get the most from their education. The school is known to be its approach to the education which meets the National educational criteria. School has fully automated office and online teaching and lesson delivery system which keeps teachers, parents, students and management connected. Our academy has given exceptional results in the academic sphere and its students have excelled in extra co-curricular activities too.

    WHAT WE DO

    We strive to develop life-long learners with the ability to apply academic, practical, and creative knowledge to real life experiences and challenges. That is why we believe that learning is most effective when blended with outside experiences that broaden and deepen students learning and interests. Together with all teachers, management and parents too, we provide all the support which a student needs. Every student is being kept well informed about upcoming tests, exams, and other competitions. Career counselling and regular motivational programmes keep our students well informed and confidant.
