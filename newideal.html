    WELCOME TO OUR SCHOOL

    Our Academy was established in 2011 by the founder of this academy Sanjay tiwari and pankaj kumar. This school runs under Ideal academy educational & welfare trust. Ideal Academy is located at Block more Barun, Aurangabad (Bihar). It is a co-educational school, with a team of more than 32 dedicated and professional faculties, which are here to ensure that the children get the most from their education. With the perfect student teacher ratio kids love to come to school. The school takes pride of its excellent teaching methodology. The school provides education to students from class I to VIII. The current student strength of this school is approximately more than 500. This school's library has more than 300 books. The school is known to be its approach to the education which meets the National educational criteria. School has fully automated office and online teaching and lesson delivery system through which teachers parents students and management remains connected. The school has given exceptional results in the academic sphere and its students have excelled in extra co-curricular activities too.

    The school is a co-educational English medium secondary school recognized by the Directorate of Education and affiliated to CENTRAL BOARD OF SECONDARY EDUCATION under all India Pattern.
